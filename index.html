Welcome to PakServe

How to Post a Task

Login to supertasker and tell us about your task in detail, mention your task whether it's physical or online when and where you want to get it done. Then add your budget for the task; you can post any task from plumbing, home cleaning to web development only in three simple steps for free.

Choose The Expert Worker for Your Task

The task seekers bid on your task, choose the best candidate by checking their profiles, reviews, and recommendations. When you accept an offer your payment will be held securely in supertasker account till the task is completed. once you accept the offer than you can message directly to the tasker to discuss in detail about your task.

Task Completed

When your task is completed you just need to release payment held with the supertasker account or if you choose for Cash on delivery option, pay your tasker in cash when the task is done and leave a review so people can know about the tasker skills.

Frequently Asked Question

You May Ask

What is Pak Serve?
PakServe is a trusted online community, a website where people can find solution for all their needs. pakServe connects people who need to outsource their tasks with the people who are looking to earn extra money and ready to work.
Who is a Tasker?
A person who can complete tasks for other people within the supertasker community is a Tasker.
How do I post a task?
Its totally free to post a task on PakServe and get offers for your task. Click on post task, add title of your task then add task description be sure to be clear what is that you need to done. Add information about task as much as possible so tasker can understand about your task. Select nature of your task you want help online or in person (physically) add date and time when and where you want to done your task. Set fair budget. Within few minutes you will start receiving offers on your post from trusted taskers you just need to choose the best candidate to complete your task.
Who is a Poster?
A poster is a person who is looking help to complete their task, whether the task is in-person or online.
